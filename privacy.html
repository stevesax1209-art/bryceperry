Privacy Policy

BrycePerry.org respects your privacy. Any personal information submitted through this website, including contact forms or newsletter sign-ups, will only be used to respond to inquiries or provide requested updates.

Information collected may include your name, email address, and message content when submitting a form.

This information will never be sold, shared, or distributed to third parties except when necessary to deliver requested services such as email communications.
