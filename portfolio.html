Health Technology

Parkinson's Community Network (PCN) App

Born from the gaps Bryce experienced as a patient, the PCN App is a patient-designed mobile tool that makes daily Parkinson's management less overwhelming and more connected. Features include intelligent medication tracking with reminders, daily symptom and mood logging, in-app community access, curated resource library, and an AI-powered support assistant trained on Parkinson's-specific information.
